# 🌾 AI-Powered Agricultural Decision & Supply Chain Optimizer

A software-based decision intelligence system that helps farmers maximize profit by optimizing market selection, pricing, and transportation using data-driven insights.

---

## 📌 Overview

Farmers often sell crops at suboptimal prices due to lack of market awareness and inefficient supply chain decisions.  
This system provides **intelligent recommendations** on where, when, and how to sell crops to maximize profit.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- 📊 **Quality & Risk Prediction**
  - Estimates crop quality based on storage, time, and environmental conditions  
  - Predicts spoilage risk  

- 💰 **Price Intelligence**
  - Compares prices across multiple markets  
  - Maps quality grades to expected pricing  

- 🚚 **Transport Optimization**
  - Calculates logistics cost  
  - Compares multiple transport options  

- 🤖 **Decision Engine (Core)**
  - Computes net profit  
  - Recommends best selling strategy
